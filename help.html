Help

This page answers common questions about using Papis Canvas Puzzles. If you need further assistance, you can contact us at the bottom of the page.

Getting Started

  • Select a puzzle from the Home screen.
  • Choose the number of pieces.
  • Drag pieces from the tray to the board to complete the puzzle.

Using My Pictures

The My Pictures section lets you create puzzles from your own photos.

  • Tap Select Images to choose photos from your device.
  • Selected photos are stored locally on your device.
  • You can delete a photo at any time by long-pressing it and choosing Delete.

Subscriptions

A subscription unlocks access to premium features, including using your own pictures.

  • Subscriptions are managed by Apple through the App Store.
  • If you cancel a purchase, the app will continue to work normally.
  • If you have an active subscription and access is locked, try Restore Purchases in Settings.

Privacy

Your privacy is important:

  • You choose which photos to use.
  • Photos remain on your device.
  • We do not upload, collect, or view your photos.

Common Questions

  • Why can’t I access My Pictures?
    My Pictures is a premium feature and requires an active subscription.
  • How do I change the app theme?
    Open the Admin tab and tap the Theme button.
  • Where are my saved puzzles?
    Open the In Progress tab to continue saved puzzles.

Contact Support

If you need help or want to report a problem, contact: papi@papiscanvas.com
