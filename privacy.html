Privacy Policy

Effective date:

Papis Canvas Puzzles (“the App”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what data is (and is not) collected when you use the App.

Information We Collect

User-Selected Photos:
The App allows you to select photos from your device in order to create personal jigsaw puzzles. Photo selection is entirely optional and initiated by you.

  • Selected photos are stored locally on your device.
  • Photos are not uploaded to any server.
  • Photos are not shared with third parties.
  • Photos are not linked to your identity.

You can delete your photos at any time from within the App.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • No account information
  • No names, email addresses, or identifiers
  • No location data
  • No contacts or address book data
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No analytics or tracking data

Subscriptions & Payments

The App offers optional subscriptions processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store your payment information. All transactions are handled directly by Apple.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data. User-selected photos remain on your device unless you choose to remove them.

Children’s Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe that personal information has been provided in error, please contact us and we will take appropriate action.

Your Control

You control all data stored by the App:

  • You decide which photos to select
  • You may delete photos at any time
  • You may remove the App to delete all stored data

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated to reflect changes to the App. Any updates will be published on this page.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact: papi@papiscanvas.com
